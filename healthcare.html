Your Well-being on Taniti

Medical Facilities

Taniti has one modern hospital located in Taniti City, equipped with emergency services and multilingual staff fluent in English, Spanish, Japanese, and more. Several clinics are available throughout the island for minor ailments and routine care.

Emergency Contacts

  • Emergency Services (Police, Fire, Ambulance): Dial 112
  • Taniti General Hospital: +1 (555) 123-4567
  • 24-Hour Pharmacy: Located next to the hospital

Travel Insurance

Visitors are encouraged to have travel insurance that covers medical expenses. Check with your provider to ensure coverage while on Taniti.

Safety Tips

  • Stay hydrated and use sunscreen to protect against the tropical sun.
  • Be cautious when participating in outdoor activities; follow safety guidelines.
  • Respect wildlife and maintain a safe distance from animals.
