Discover Our Attractions

Beaches

Taniti boasts miles of pristine white sandy beaches with crystal-clear waters perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports. Whether you're looking to relax under the sun or engage in activities like snorkeling and paddleboarding, our beaches offer something for everyone.

  • White Sands Beach: Ideal for families and picnics.
  • Coral Cove: Perfect for snorkeling and diving.
  • Sunset Bay: Enjoy breathtaking sunsets every evening.

Rainforest Adventures

Explore the lush tropical rainforests of Taniti, home to diverse flora and fauna. Join guided hikes to discover hidden waterfalls, exotic wildlife, and the serene beauty of nature untouched by time.

  • Guided Hikes: Led by experienced local guides.
  • Zip-Lining: Glide through the canopy for a thrilling experience.
  • Bird Watching: Spot rare and endemic bird species.
View on Map

Volcano Tours

Visit Taniti's active volcano for an unforgettable adventure. Guided tours take you safely to vantage points where you can witness the awe-inspiring power of nature. Night tours offer a spectacular view of glowing lava flows.

  • Day Tours: Learn about the geology and history.
  • Night Tours: Experience the volcanic glow.
  • Photography Expeditions: Capture stunning landscapes.
